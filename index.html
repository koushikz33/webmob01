3 best cheap food options around TIU Ikebukuro Campus

Koushik Kanti Saha • Created at May 6th, 2024

1

Thai Street Food

Get Directions

I went there a lot of times to try their delicious Thai menu. It was really cheap and you could get a full meal around 1500yen. Other high end Thai places would cost over 3000 yen. So this place instead is awesome! Try it!

Recommendation: Tare Pad Pong Curry

2

Efe Kebab

Get Directions

Delicious Kebab only for 500yen! Everything is 500yen! except one item (I forgot the name) which was 350yen! Go try!

Recommendation: Kebab Don with Mid Spicy Sauce

3

Shakey's

Get Directions

Italian buffet with a wide range of options! Try everything for 2150yen / 3hrs!

Recommendation: Everything!
